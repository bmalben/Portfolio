About

Harry Styles

Born: February 1, 1994, in Redditch, Worcestershire, England
Occupation: Singer, songwriter, actor, and model
Known for: Former member of the British boy band One Direction, successful solo music career, androgynous fashion sense, and acting roles in films like "Dunkirk" and "Don't Worry Darling".
Physical Description: Tall and slender young man with curly hair, piercing green eyes, and a charming smile. Has a collection of tattoos and often experiments with fashion style, blending masculine and feminine elements.
Career Highlights: Released debut solo album "Harry Styles" in 2017, which included the hit single "Sign of the Times". Second album "Fine Line" was released in 2019 and debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. Has also modeled for Gucci and appeared on the covers of numerous fashion magazines.
